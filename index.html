Existing Problem

  • We don't get things done, due to:
    • We waste a LOT of time procrastinating, on social media, or on video games!
    • The lack of time we have as we're busy in work, university, or school
  • And as a result of this, many of us are unemployed or working in some exhausted jobs for a little wage!

Our Solution

  • “5-Minute of learning today is better than none!”
    (1․01)365 = 37․8
  • We Share One Little Achievement Every Day!
    So little that you could do it while the time of drinking your daily cup of coffee or tea, and the mindset will be changed as you'll say "I'll do that then will do whatever I want that is wasting my time"!
  • We're not a social media platform, we are more like a blog or a diary of achievments (website & app) where we help people achieve their goals, be more active & productive, and master their crafts.
  • More about us, and the frontend design

Value Proposition

  • Making productivity a daily habit
  • Save users' little achievements and track their days
  • Challenging through the active community and the badges
  • Change youth's future
  • Simple and not wasting the time

Customer Segment

  • High school, and university students
  • Anyone has a goal and want to achieve it "in mostly any field"
  • Good level in English, has a smart phone or pc, and has internet connection

How we'll get revenue?

  • From Bootcamps (next phase); you can see how it works on the about page

    - Plan (b) we could make classes page where mentors can make ads for their online courses, or classes on Slack or facebook group, etc ... and we'll take money for each day

    - And plan (c) We could make dynamically bootcamp page, where it'll contain a quiz and number of projects (like a university class), and after submitting he'll be able to see others submits, and we could compensate the lack of engagement with leaderboard for each bootcamp and this will be public even if user is not joining that bootcamp.
